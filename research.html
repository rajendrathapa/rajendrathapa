Research

Current Research

  • Compuatational modeling of laser induced changes in glasses
    • Design a pipeline for regression algorithm to classify atoms as crystal-like and non-cyrstal like based on suitable features
    • Design a pipeline for unsupervised machine learning using k-means clustering to track the evolution of cluster sizes during crystallization
    • Using simulation, perform parameter optimization of various laser paramters to be used in experiments
  • Atomic scale modeling of seeded crystal growth in glasses
    • Use KMeans Clustering algorithm to classify atoms as glass-like or crystal-like based on static and dynamic feature of each atom
    • Use stochastic modeling to predict probabilities of formation of seeds at random locations inside glass
    • Perform time-series analysis of various material properties under high temperature annealing

PhD Research

  • Ab initio simulation of amorphous graphite
    • Implement DFT and ML force-fields to model formation of amorphous graphite under high temperature annealing
    • Used different techniques to study time-series of layers of disordered graphite formation
  • FEAR modeling of various systems (zirconia-tantala, metallic glasses, Ge-Se chalcogenide)
  • Simulation of various layered carbon structures using Gaussian Approximation Potential
