About Me

As a computational physicist and data scientist, I thrive on combining the power of numerical simulations with data-driven insights to unravel the mysteries of the universe. With a background in both physics and machine learning, I bring a unique perspective to complex problems, seeking elegant solutions through interdisciplinary approaches.

My journey began during my studies in theoretical physics, where I delved into simulating quantum systems and analyzing their behavior. Fascinated by the potential of data, I expanded my skill set to include machine learning and data analysis, enabling me to extract meaningful patterns from experimental observations and model predictions.

Today, I collaborate with researchers across domains, harnessing the power of algorithms to advance our understanding of natural phenomena. When I'm not immersed in code or datasets, I enjoy sharing my passion for science through talks, workshops, and writing. Whether it's exploring the cosmos or dissecting intricate datasets, I'm dedicated to pushing boundaries and fostering innovation.
